# rushdAcademy-education-backend
Advanced educational and training platform dedicated to unlocking potential and building a new generation of skilled, market-ready professionals, with a particular focus on supporting and developing vital skills in technology, design, and languages.
The academy strives to bridge the gap between traditional academic education and market demands, transforming ambitious students into "builders and developers" who can create value, take responsibility, and grow into leaders and mentors within their communities.

## Skills:
PHP - Laravel – MySQL – JavaScript – Api - localisation - deployment

## Deliverables:
Centralised Dashboard: Manage students, instructors, and courses in one place.
Automated Processes: Simplify registration, attendance, and daily operations.
User Guide: Easy instructions for system management and progress tracking
